Meta unveils "Segment Anything" AI image identification tool, but the scale of the dataset used by the social giant raises privacy concerns.

With its Metaverse ambitions in shambles, Meta is now looking to AI to drive its next stage of development. One of Meta’s latest projects, the social media giant announced on Wednesday, is called the Segment Anything Model. Segment Anything helps users identify specific items in an image with a few clicks. While still in demo mode, the company says Segment Anything can already take a photo and individually identify the pixels comprising everything in the picture so that one or more items can be separated from the rest of the image.
